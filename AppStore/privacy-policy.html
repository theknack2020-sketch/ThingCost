Privacy Policy

ThingCost · Effective Date: March 16, 2026

Overview

ThingCost is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

Data Storage

All item data (names, prices, purchase dates, categories) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Collection

ThingCost does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising data

In-App Purchases

ThingCost offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited items. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

ThingCost does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchase processing.

Sharing Feature

ThingCost allows you to generate and share cost cards via the iOS share sheet. These images are created locally on your device and shared only when you explicitly choose to do so through iOS sharing mechanisms. We have no access to shared content.

Children's Privacy

ThingCost does not collect any data from any user, including children. The app is safe for use by people of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
