1. APP FUNCTIONALITY (with screen recording description):
ThingCost launches to a 4-page onboarding flow. After onboarding, the user lands on the Items tab with an empty state. Tap "+" to add items (name, price, purchase date, category). The app calculates and displays the daily cost of each item.

Core user flow:
- Add Item → Enter name + price + date + category → Tap "Add"
- Items List → Summary card shows total daily cost, streak, monthly cost
- Tap any item → Detail view with Worth It Score (0-100), cost chart, future projections
- Share button → Generate social media share card (3 styles: Minimal, Bold, Gradient)
- Swipe left → Delete item, Swipe right → Edit item
- Dashboard tab → Category breakdown chart, Worth It Overview, Most Expensive list
- Settings tab → Appearance (System/Light/Dark), Notifications, Legal links, Cross-promo

No account system. No login required. No user-generated content. No camera, microphone, or location access. No prompts requesting sensitive data.

Purchase flow: Free users can track up to 5 items. When attempting to add a 6th item, a paywall appears offering a one-time Non-Consumable purchase ("ThingCost Pro") via StoreKit 2. "Restore Purchases" is available on the paywall and in Settings.

2. APP PURPOSE & VALUE:
ThingCost helps users understand the true daily cost of their purchases. Users enter an item name, price, and purchase date — the app calculates the daily cost (price ÷ days owned), which decreases every day. The unique "Worth It Score" (0-100) rates each purchase based on ownership duration and daily cost trajectory.

This helps users make more mindful purchasing decisions by visualizing how cost amortizes over time. Example: a $1,000 phone bought a year ago costs $2.74/day — and dropping.

3. REVIEW INSTRUCTIONS:
- Launch → Complete 4-page onboarding (tap "Next" three times, then "Skip" or "Add Your First Item")
- On Items tab, tap "+" to add items (enter any name, price, and date)
- Tap any item row to see detail view with Worth It Score, cost chart, and future projections
- Tap share icon on detail view to see share card preview
- Swipe left on item to delete, swipe right to edit
- Tap Dashboard tab for analytics overview
- Tap Settings tab for Appearance, Notifications, Legal, Restore Purchases

No login or test credentials required. All features are accessible without an account.
