Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

ThingCost ("the App") is developed by TheKnack. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

Data Collection

All information you enter in ThingCost (item names, prices, purchase dates, photos) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to your personal data.

Analytics

ThingCost uses TelemetryDeck for privacy-friendly, anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal data, device identifiers, or IP addresses. It uses differential privacy techniques to provide aggregated insights (such as which screens are most visited) without identifying individual users. No advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking is used. TelemetryDeck is fully GDPR and CCPA compliant.

No Tracking

The App does not use advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or any technology that requires App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission. We do not collect device identifiers or behavioral profiles.

Third-Party Services

  • TelemetryDeck — Anonymous, privacy-first analytics (see above)
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share a cost card or CSV export using the system share sheet.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, all associated data is permanently removed. The App does not use cloud backup or sync.

Children's Privacy

ThingCost does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or personal information of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@theknack.dev.
