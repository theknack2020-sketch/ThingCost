Frequently Asked Questions

How does ThingCost calculate daily cost?

Daily cost = purchase price ÷ number of days you've owned the item. The longer you own something, the lower its daily cost becomes.

What is the Worth It Score?

The Worth It Score rates every purchase from 0 to 100 based on how long you've owned it, how often you use it, and how much the daily cost has dropped. Scores above 80 are "Amazing," while below 40 means it might not have been worth it.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. Nothing is uploaded to any server. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. This will restore your Pro unlock if you previously purchased it on the same Apple ID.

What's included in ThingCost Pro?

Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription) that unlocks:

  • Unlimited items (free tier: 5 items)
  • Item photos
  • Use tracking & cost per use
  • All share card styles
  • All themes
  • Advanced charts
  • CSV export
  • Unlimited future projections
  • Custom categories
  • All widget sizes
  • Priority support

Can I transfer my data to a new device?

Currently, ThingCost stores data locally and does not support cloud sync or device transfer. This feature is planned for a future update.

How do I delete an item?

On the Items tab, swipe left on any item and tap Delete. This cannot be undone.

The app is in the wrong language. How do I change it?

ThingCost follows your device language. Go to Settings → General → Language & Region to change your device language.
